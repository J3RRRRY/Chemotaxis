Random Walk of 5 Bacteria

Left Click With Mouse To Make the Bacteria move to the Left or Right.

(Hold Left CLick on the Left Half of the Screen to go Left and Left Click on the Right Half to go Right)

Right Click With Mouse To Make the Bacteria move Up or Down.

(Hold Right CLick on the Top Half of the Screen to go Up and Right Click on the Bottom Half to go Down)
